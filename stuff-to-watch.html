Betty Boop: Snow White

4/4/2018

 
A superb re-telling of the story of Snow White by the Fleischer Studios. Cab Calloway, no stranger in the world of Betty Boop, joins in, as Koko the Clown, and sings along. A milestone of The Golden Age of American animation.
